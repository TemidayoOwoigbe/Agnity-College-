About Us

Committed to Education

Agnity College was founded with one purpose - to build a space for all students to grow, learn and create with each passing day. Through a unique teaching approach and a truly passionate staff, we help students develop academically and personally to the highest level.
We invite you to explore our site and discover the academics and community Agnity College provides to each and every student. Get in touch with us today to schedule a tour, learn more about enrollment, or ask any other questions.

Mission Statement

Tomorrow Starts Now

Agnity College is committed to providing a comprehensive and inclusive secondary education to all students, fostering their academic, personal, and social development in a supportive and challenging environment. We aim to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in a rapidly changing world and to become responsible and active members of society. Through a rich and diverse curriculum, innovative teaching methods, and collaboration with families and communities, Agnity College empowers students to reach their full potential and to make a positive impact in the world.

Philosophy and Vision Statement

Empowering Students for a Better Future

At Agnity College, we believe in educating the whole student – academically, socially, and emotionally – to prepare them for a fulfilling and successful future. Our vision is to provide a dynamic and inclusive learning environment where students can develop their full potential, embrace diversity, and become responsible and engaged members of society. We are dedicated to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning, and to supporting students as they grow and evolve into independent, confident, and ethical individuals. Our goal is to create a school community where students are inspired to achieve their best and to make a positive impact in the world.
